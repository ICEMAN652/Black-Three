🃏 Black Three

Kala Teen – Indian Card Game (up to 6 players)

How to Play

  • 48 cards dealt (8 each), no 2s in the deck
  • Empty seats are filled by bots.
  • Bidding: Seat 1 opens at 170. Bid higher or pass.
  • Bid winner picks a trump suit and calls 2 partner cards. Holders are secret allies.
  • 8 tricks played. Must follow suit; trump beats all.
  • Points: Ace = 20, K/Q/J/10 = 10, ♠3 = 30, others = 0
  • Bidder's team needs ≥ bid pts. Win: bidder ×2 pts, partners × bid. Lose: opponents each earn bid.
