🃏 Black Three

Kala Teen – Indian Card Game (6 players)

How to Play

  • 48 cards dealt (8 each), no 2s in the deck
  • You are Player 1. Five AI opponents play against / with you.
  • Bidding: You must open at 170. Bid higher or pass each round.
  • Bid winner picks a trump suit and calls 2 partner cards (cards they don't hold). The holders are secret allies.
  • 8 tricks are played. Must follow suit if possible; trump beats all.
  • Points: Ace = 20, K/Q/J/10 = 10, ♠3 = 30, others = 0
  • Bidder's team wins if they collect ≥ bid points. Bidder earns bid×2, each partner earns bid.
  • If bidder's team fails, the three opponents each earn bid points.
